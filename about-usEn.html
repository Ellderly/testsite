WHAT IS KLIVER_POLSKA DOING?

Development and production of scientific and technical procurement, namely electronic-optical devices devices for monitoring the surrounding air, ground, surface and underwater environments, including sound-acoustic active and passive systems. Also to these products include automatic, mobile underwater vehicles (AUVs) designed to monitor underwater mobile objects and objects located on the seabed. Using optical and hydroacoustic systems. These works have a connection with the main ship which carried out through optical fiber cables and radio communication means through the connected to such an AUV buoy
