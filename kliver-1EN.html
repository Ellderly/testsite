Stationary system of passive monitoring of sea water area “Kliver-Polska 1”

  • Reception, processing and classification of acoustic signals for detection underwater objects
  • Detection of surface objects;
  • Coastal barrier line of the underwater surveillance system;
  • Round-the-clock surveillance under water;
  • Protection of the coast, naval bases and ports, oil platforms and water areas that require special attention;
  • Monitoring of tsunami threats, monitoring of currents, temperature and salinity

Характеристики місця розташування систем

  • The depth of the sea at the location of the station is from 40 to 200 m.
  • The intensity of swimming in a zone with a radius of 4 km from the installation point stations, a maximum of three vessels at the same time.

характеристика сучасних малошумних підводних човнів, виявляємих назькочастотним каналом

  • speed of movement
  • not less than 2m/s (4 knots)
  • Water capacity
  • not less than 1000t
  • Reduced noise level of underwater target to 1m
  • not less than 0.05 Pa per 1 kHz in the Hz range
  • Environmental noise spectrum level in the working range of 10 - 100 Hz
  • not less than 0.1 Ra at each of the frequencies

Main Features

  • Accuracy of setting the object with errors that do not exceed the characteristics listed above
  • +-5 (confirmed by test results)
  • Range for an object with the above characteristics
  • not less than 4km (confirmed by test results)
  • Probability of correctly classifying the target as an underwater object or a surface vessel
  • not less than 0.95 (confirmed by test results)
  • Transfer of information to the shore station
  • It is carried out using a connection: underwater sound; radio Buick; fiber optic;
  • Range of radio transmissions from the radio buoy to the shore station
  • at least 50 km (calculated depending on the height of the location of the radio receiving antenna)
  • Battery life at the station
  • 12 months
  • System lifetime
  • 5 years, subject to annual maintenance

Marine indicators for proper operation of the station:

  • The agitation of the sea
  • do not exceed 6 points (wind from 14 m/s to 17.5 m/s, wave height from 4 to 6 m)
  • Current speed
  • up to 2 m/s
  • Sea water temperature
  • From 0"С to +35'С
  • Limited ambient temperatures to install station
  • -28'С to -50'С
