Electron-optical observation

Jura-2C

  • Matrix format
    1280 × 1024
  • Frame rate, Hz
    30...500
  • Focal distance
    40-500
  • Focal distance, mm
    1000 (1600)
The system can form up to six support strobes and accordingly observe up to 6 objects in the field of view of the lens at the same time.

Operation modes

  • Automatic mode accompanying
  • Semi-automatic mode accompanying
  • Manual mode accompanying

Mobility

The "Jura-2C" system is designed for mobile use and involves deploying, rebasing and taking measurements directly from the chassis of a high-terrain vehicle.

Discoverable objects

planes, rotorcraft on takeoff, landing and during flight;
land stationary objects and vehicles, sea and river vessels in appointed surveillance sector.
